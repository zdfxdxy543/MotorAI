◆ v_over_f

ctrl_gt ctl_const_vf_controller::v_over_f

The constant V/F ratio (Volts per Hertz).

This parameter defines the proportional relationship between voltage and frequency. The underlying electromagnetic principle is:

\[ E = 4.44 \cdot N \cdot \Phi \cdot f = V_{ratio} \cdot f \]

where \(E\) is the EMF, \(N\) is the number of turns, \(\Phi\) is the magnetic flux, and \(f\) is the frequency.
