Detailed Description

A library of discrete resonant controllers for AC signal tracking.

This file implements several variations of resonant controllers, which are essential for AC signal tracking, particularly in applications like grid-tied inverters. They provide infinite gain at a specific resonant frequency, allowing for zero steady-state error when tracking a sinusoidal reference. Implementations include pure Resonant (R), Proportional-Resonant (PR), Quasi-Resonant (QR), and Quasi-Proportional-Resonant (QPR) controllers.

Initializes a quasi-resonant controller with Frequency Pre-warping.

Corrects the frequency warping effect of bilinear transformation at the resonant frequency. Essential for harmonic control (e.g., 6th, 12th harmonics).

Parameters
[out]qrPointer to the QR controller instance.
[in]krGain of the resonant term.
[in]freq_resonantResonant frequency in Hz (Center Frequency).
[in]freq_cutCutoff frequency in Hz (Bandwidth/2).
[in]fsSampling frequency in Hz.
